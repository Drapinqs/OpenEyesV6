About Us / FAQ

What exactly is OpenEyes?

OpenEyes is a first of its kind! We're a mixture between a repair shop and an online learning platform. We offer our users the ability to get their devices repaired in-store, and encourage them to check out our learning courses.

What does OpenEyes teach?

Our online learning courses cover topics focused on technology, and will reward users with an industry-leading certificate upon completion. You can view all the courses we offer and the topics we cover over at our Learning Page.

Can OpenEyes support my business?

If you are an enterprise or business-related platform interested in using our services, please contact us through our Contact Page so that we can connect with you.
